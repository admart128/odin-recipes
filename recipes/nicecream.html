A fun and delightful twist on classic ice cream, this homemade Nice Cream is made with all-natural ingredients for a refreshing and delicious treat.

Ingredients:

  • 4 ripe bananas, sliced and frozen
  • 1/2 cup milk or dairy-free alternative
  • 1/4 cup honey or maple syrup
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • Optional: 1/4 cup cocoa powder, nut butter, or fruit puree for flavor variations
  • Optional: Toppings of your choice (chopped nuts, chocolate chips, fresh fruit, etc.)

Instructions:

  1. In a blender or food processor, combine frozen banana slices, milk, honey or maple syrup, and vanilla extract. Blend until smooth and creamy, scraping down the sides as needed. If desired, add cocoa powder, nut butter, or fruit puree for flavor variations, and blend until well incorporated.
  2. Transfer the Nice Cream to a lidded container and freeze for at least 2 hours, or until firm enough to scoop.
  3. When ready to serve, remove the Nice Cream from the freezer and let it sit at room temperature for a few minutes to soften slightly. Scoop into bowls or cones, and top with your choice of toppings. Enjoy immediately.
