Lasagna

A classic Italian comfort food, this delicious Lasagna features layers of tender pasta, savory beef, and creamy béchamel sauce.

Ingredients:

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 jar (24 oz) marinara sauce
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C). Cook lasagna noodles according to package instructions, then drain and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet, cook ground beef, onion, and garlic over medium heat until beef is browned. Stir in marinara sauce, basil, oregano, salt, and pepper. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 15 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  3. In a medium bowl, combine ricotta cheese and egg. Mix well.
  4. Spread a thin layer of meat sauce on the bottom of a 9x13-inch baking dish. Arrange a layer of noodles over the sauce, then spread a layer of the ricotta mixture over the noodles. Add another layer of meat sauce, followed by a layer of mozzarella cheese. Repeat layers, finishing with a layer of noodles topped with meat sauce, mozzarella cheese, and grated Parmesan cheese.
  5. Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes, or until cheese is melted and bubbly.
  6. Allow the lasagna to rest for 10-15 minutes before cutting and serving. Enjoy with a side of garlic bread or a fresh salad.
