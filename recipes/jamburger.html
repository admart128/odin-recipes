A playful twist on a classic hamburger, the Jamburger blends savory and sweet flavors for a deliciously unique creation.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1/4 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • 1/4 tsp garlic powder
  • 4 slices cheddar cheese
  • 4 brioche buns, toasted
  • 4 lettuce leaves
  • 4 slices tomato
  • 1/4 cup jam of your choice

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine ground beef, breadcrumbs, Parmesan cheese, egg, salt, pepper, and garlic powder. Mix until well combined.
  3. Divide the beef mixture into four equal parts and form them into patties.
  4. Grill the patties for 5-6 minutes per side or until cooked to your desired level of doneness.
  5. Top each patty with a slice of cheddar cheese and continue to grill for another minute, or until cheese is melted.
  6. Place a cooked patty on the bottom half of each toasted brioche bun. Top with lettuce, tomato, and a generous dollop of jam. Place the top half of the bun on top and serve immediately.
